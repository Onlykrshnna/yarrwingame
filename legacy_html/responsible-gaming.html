Responsible Gaming

Your safety and well-being are our top priorities. We are committed to providing a fun and safe gaming environment for all our players.

18+ Policy

Yaar Win is strictly for players aged 18 and above. We employ rigorous age verification processes to ensure that minors do not have access to our real-money skill gaming services.

Our Commitment

Skill gaming is a form of entertainment. However, we recognize that it can become a concern for some individuals. We provide tools and resources to help our players stay in control of their gaming habits.

Self-Exclusion

If you feel you need a break from gaming, you can request a self-exclusion period. During this time, your account will be suspended, and you will not receive any marketing materials from us. To initiate self-exclusion, please contact our support team.

Stay in Control

  • Gaming should be seen as entertainment, not a source of income.
  • Only play with money you can afford to lose.
  • Keep track of the time and money you spend on gaming.
  • Never chase your losses.
